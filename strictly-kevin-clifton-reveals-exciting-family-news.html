Strictly star Kevin Clifton reveals exciting family-related news

August 2024 · 3 minute read

The pro dancer's parents' dance school is about to welcome two new Strictly stars

    Kevin Clifton's family are about to collaborate with his real-life family – and all for a very special cause! The pro dancer's parents' dance school, Clifton Dance Academy, hosts an annual Christmas event, and this year, his co-stars Johannes Radebe and Nancy Xu are coming along to dance for the evening. Kevin made the exciting announcement on Instagram alongside a promo photo of the two pros. He wrote: "Massive announcement! @Johannesradebe is coming to Grimsby with @Nancy_xuxi to dance at the @Cliftonsdanceacademy86 annual xmas party! Get your tickets now!" The party is taking place on 16 December at Humber Royal Hall in Grimsby, and what's more, fans have the chance to buy a ticket and join in the fun.
