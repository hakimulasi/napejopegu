Ubah Hassan Biography and Career Highlights

Ubah Hassan, born on August 27, 1987, in Somalia, is a Somali-Canadian model known for her striking features and unique look. She began her modeling career at the age of 17 after being scouted by a photographer in Canada.

Hassan quickly signed with Click Model Management in 2007 and has since worked with prestigious brands like Burberry, Vogue Italia, and Harper’s Bazaar. She made history as the first black model to appear in a Burberry campaign in 2008. In addition to her modeling success, she has been featured on Forbes’ “Africa’s Most Successful Women” list and is an advocate for diversity and representation in the fashion industry.
