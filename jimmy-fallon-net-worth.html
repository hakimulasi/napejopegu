FAQs about Jimmy Fallon

What is Jimmy Fallon known for?

He is known for his role as a cast member on Saturday Night Live from 1998 to 2004 and as the host of The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon since 2014.

Did Fallon go to college?

Yes, he attended The College of Saint Rose in Albany, New York, but dropped out to pursue comedy. He later received an honorary degree from the college in 2009.

How did he start his comedy career?

He began his comedy career performing stand-up at various clubs and eventually moved to Los Angeles at the age of 21 to pursue comedy full-time. He joined the cast of SNL in 1998 after impressing the show’s creator with his impressions during his audition.

Has Fallon won any awards?

What are some popular segments on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon?

Some popular segments include Thank You Notes, Egg Russian Roulette, and Lip Sync Battle, which became so popular it led to its own spin-off show.

Is Jimmy Fallon married?

Yes, he is married to film producer Nancy Juvonen. They married in December 2007 and have two daughters.

What are some films he has appeared in?

Fallon has appeared in films such as Taxi, Fever Pitch, and Almost Famous.

Does he have any musical talents?

Yes, he is an accomplished musician who plays guitar and drums and often incorporates music into his shows.

What are some of Fallon’s comedic influences?

His favorite comedians include Eddie Murphy, Jerry Seinfeld, and Steve Martin.
