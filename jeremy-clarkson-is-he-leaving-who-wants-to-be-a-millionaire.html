Jeremy Clarkson: Who Is He? Biography

Jeremy Clarkson is a name synonymous with motoring journalism, and for a good reason. With his bold opinions and controversial takes on various car brands, Clarkson has made a name for himself in the field of motoring journalism.3

Early Life and Career

Born and raised in Doncaster, England, Clarkson’s early interest in cars led him to pursue a career in the field.

However, his teenage years were not without their fair share of struggles. After being expelled from Repton Public School for bad behavior, Clarkson joined his parents’ business of selling toys.

It was in 1984 that Clarkson founded the ‘Motoring Press Agency,’ which proved to be a stepping stone for his career in motoring journalism.

His work caught the attention of major publications like The Sun and The Sunday Times, who approached him to write motor reviews for them.

Rise to Fame

Clarkson’s success truly took off after he became a presenter on BBC’s Top Gear in 1988. His no-nonsense approach to reviewing cars and his penchant for being unapologetically honest about his opinions won him a loyal following.

However, Clarkson’s time at Top Gear was not without controversy. His opinions on various car brands often drew criticism, and his outspoken personality landed him in hot water more than once.

Despite the controversies, Clarkson remained an integral part of Top Gear’s success until his departure in 2015.
