Queer Eye's Tom confirms he has reconciled with Abby and the Internet can't handle it

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Tom was the main subject of the makeover in the first episode of Queer Eye

    For those who have yet to watch it, Netflix's has proved extremely popular with viewers and critics alike. The show, which is a reboot of , follows a team of experts as they makeover a new person each episode, helping them with their physical appearance as well as their personal struggles. Viewers were particularly fond of Tom, a 57-year-old man who joked that the group couldn't "fix ugly", and ended up delighted by his transformation. The end of the episode saw him invite his ex-wife, who he revealed he was still in love with, out to a car show, and to see his newly-decorated home, so fans were thrilled when Tom announced on Twitter that the pair had officially reconciled.

    Tom loved his makeover 

    Sharing a snap of himself with his arm around Abby, Tom wrote: "So happy to say that Abby and I are reunited  we’ve loved each other for many years and want to spend the rest of our lives together!" Fans were quick to respond, with one writing: "I am embarrassed at how happy this announcement makes me!! Congratulations," while another wrote: "Tom, that's such great news.  I loved watching the @QueerEye episode that featured you, and I hoped that Abby and you would find the happiness that you both clearly deserve. All the best from a faraway fan."
