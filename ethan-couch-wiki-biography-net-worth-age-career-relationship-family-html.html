  • Ethan Couch gained notoriety for his involvement in a tragic drink-drive crash on June 15, 2013, in Burleson, Texas. The incident resulted in the death of four individuals and the injury of nine others. Ethan, often referred to as the “Affluenza teen,” has infamously blamed his privileged upbringing for his actions.
  • According to his lawyer, Ethan claimed that he was unable to distinguish between right and wrong due to his wealthy background. As a result, he received a sentence of 10 years in prison. However, he served only two years before being released from the Tarrant County jail near Dallas on April 2, 2018.

Ethan Couch: Biography, Wiki, Age & Education

  • Ethan Anthony Couch was born on April 11, 1997, in Burleson, Texas, USA. As of 2018, he is approximately 21 years old. His father, Fred Couch, is a businessperson, and his mother’s name is Tonya Couch. Unfortunately, information about his siblings is currently unavailable.
  • Ethan attended Anderson Private School for his schooling. He also enrolled in a community college, but due to the drink-drive crash incident, he was unable to continue his studies.

Ethan Couch: Net worth, Wealth, Money & House

  • Prior to the incident, Ethan worked as an employee at a sheet metal company. However, his wealth and assets are primarily derived from his father’s business. It is estimated that he has a net worth of 11.7 million US dollars (as of 2018). Additionally, he owns a lavish wooden house in Burleson, Texas, valued at 21 million US dollars.
