Hilary Duff Early Life

The second of two children, Hilary Duff was born on September 28, 1987 in Houston, Texas. Haylie Duff, her older sister, would also go on to become an actress.

Hilary and Haylie both took singing, acting, and ballet classes when they were little. Local theatre productions resulted from this. In 1993, the sisters and their mother relocated to Los Angeles in order to pursue additional acting chances. They entered the audition circuit right away and soon each secured a number of ads.

Hilary was cast in a small, uncredited role in the 1997 season of True Women, a Hallmark Entertainment television program. She received her first significant leading role in the 1998 film Casper Meets Wendy. At the end of the 1990s, she continued to land more film and television roles.

Hilary Duff Career

Duff debuted in the titular role of the television series “Lizzie McGuire” in 2001, which would go on to become her most well-known work. She would eventually make an appearance in 65 episodes of the show. She had an appearance in the show’s theatrical picture in 2003. Hilary would executive produce a relaunch of the show for Disney +, it was revealed in August 2019.

Disney made almost $100 million from just the show’s retail sales during its existence. For the entire series, Duff made a little under $1 million, or $15,000 per episode of Lizzie McGuire.
In the same year, she made an additional $1 million as a co-star in “Cheaper by the Dozen.” She would receive $2 million in 2005 for her role in the follow-up to that film.

In addition to $2 million each for “Raise Your Voice,” “The Perfect Man,” and “Material Girls,” Duff got $2.2 million for “A Cinderella Story.”

Duff has had numerous guest appearances on television shows, such as “Gossip Girl,” “Beauty & the Briefcase,” and “Younger.” Additionally, she has acted in a number of independent movies, such as Bloodworth, According to Greta, and War, Inc.

Hilary Duff Personal Life

Before he met and began dating Lindsay Lohan, Duff dated pop singer Aaron Carter intermittently between 2001 and 2003. The couple’s age difference garnered media attention, and two years later, in November 2006, they split up.

Mike Comrie, a Canadian NHL player, and Duff started dating in 2007. They were wed between 2010 and 2016. Luca Cruz Comrie, the son of the couple, was born in March 2012. Mike comes from a very prosperous family. A Canadian furniture and electronics conglomerate called The Brick, which the family sold for $700 million, gave his father Bill Comrie a net worth of $500 million. The couple announced their separation in January 2014 but stated that they would still co-parent their son.

Since the beginning of 2017, Hilary has been dating musician, songwriter, and producer Matthew Koma. In October 2018, they welcomed a child together. Matthew is primarily known for penning hit songs for the musician Zedd. In December 2019, Hilary and Matthew exchanged vows in a private ceremony at their home.
