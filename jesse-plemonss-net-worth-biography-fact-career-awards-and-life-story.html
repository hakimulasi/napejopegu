Jesse Plemons net worth and salary: Jesse Plemons is an American actor who has a net worth of $8 million dollars. Jesse Plemons was born in Dallas, Texas on April 2, 1988. He grew up in Mart, Texas, a small town outside of Waco. He began his professional acting career in 1998, when he was ten years old, thought he did appear in a Coca-Cola commercial when he was 3 years old.

Early Career: He made his debut in the 1998 film, “Finding North”. The following year he appeared in the teen football classic “Varsity Blues”. Over the next several years he appeared in a number of film and television roles, including “All the Pretty Horses”, “Walker, Texas Ranger”, “The Guardian”, “Sabrina, The Teenage Witch”, “Like Mike”, “When Zachary Beaver Came to Town”, “Judging Amy”, “Huff”, “CSI: Crime Scene Investigation”, “Grey’s Anatomy”, “NCIS”, “Spy Kids”, “Observe and Report”, “Fear Itself”, “Cold Case”, “Meeting Spencer”, “Paul”, “The Master”, “Children’s Hospital”, and “Bent”.

In 2006 he debuted in the role that would put him on the map, as Landry Clarke on “Friday Nights Lights”. He appeared in 59 episodes of the series between 2006 and 2011.

In 2014 he debuted in another role that cemented his status, as Todd Alquist in “Breaking Bad”. He would eventually appear in 11 episodes of the television series and was brought back in a larger way for the 2019 Netflix film “El Camino: A Breaking Bad Movie”.

Another critically acclaimed role came in 2015 when he appeared as Ed Blumquist in the FX series “Fargo”. This role would win Jesse a Critics’ Choice Award for Best Supporting Actor in a Movie/Miniseries.

In 2017 he appeared in a fan-favorite episode of the dark drama “Black Mirror”, for which he was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award.

Other notable films and television projects include 2018’s “Game Night” and 2019’s “The Irishman”. Jesse has also been floated for a starring role in a future Star Wars film.

Personal Life: Jesse met Kirsten Dunst for the first time on the set of Fargo in 2015. At the time they were both in separate relationships. In 2016 they began dating. They became engaged in 2017 and welcomed a baby in 2018.

Comparison to Matt Damon: For better or worse, Jesse is often noted for his resemblance to Matt Damon. He is sometimes derisively referred to as “Fat Damon”. After appearing in Breaking Bad, some referred to him as “Meth Damon”. Interestingly, Jesse actually played a younger version of Matt Damon in the 2000 movie “All the Pretty Horses. He has also been said to resemble Philip Seymour Hoffman. In 2012 he played Hoffman’s son in the movie “The Master”.
