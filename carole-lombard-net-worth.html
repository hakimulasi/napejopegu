FAQs About Carole Lombard

What was Carole Lombard’s real name?

Her real name was Jane Alice Peters. She changed it to Carole Lombard early in her career to make it more distinctive.

How did she get her start in acting?

She was discovered at the age of 12 while playing baseball in the street by director Allan Dwan, who cast her in the film A Perfect Crime (1921).

What are some of her most famous movies?

She is best known for her roles in Twentieth Century (1934), My Man Godfrey (1936), and To Be or Not to Be (1942). These films established her as a leading actress in screwball comedies.

Her comedic style was a mix of sophistication and earthiness, which allowed her to play a wide range of characters from glamorous socialites to zany, down-to-earth women.
