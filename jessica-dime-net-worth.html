What is Jessica Dime's Net Worth?

Jessica Dime is an American rapper who has a net worth of $200 thousand. She is best known for appearing on the hit VH1 reality series, Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta, but has also recorded several albums throughout her musical career. Known for her full head of pink hair, and no nonsense attitude, Dimepiece (as her fans refer to her), brings an interesting twist to the cast of Love & Hip Hop.

She was born in Tennessee as Jessica Chatman, and later moved to Miami, Florida.  She started out at the world-famous gentlemen's club, King Of Diamonds, in Miami and later began modeling on the covers of magazines, and appearing in music videos by artists like Rick Ross, Waka Flocka, Gucci Mane, Yo Gotti, and Trina. She eventually relocated to Atlanta with the goal of becoming the next Nikki Minaj; she was signed by international superstar, Flo Rida's management company in 2013, but decided to part ways a year later. In 2013, she was arrested in in Shelby County, Tennessee and charged with a felony class C count of robbery.
