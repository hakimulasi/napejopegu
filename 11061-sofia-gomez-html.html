Who's Sofia Gomez?

She's an American content creator and Internet celebrity. She was born on June 30, 2002, in Florida.

Who are Sofia Gomez's parents?

Sofia prefers to keep her private life out of the limelight. The information about her family members is scarce. Fans only know that she has a sister named Bella. In 2021, she shared her dad's photo on Instagram and confessed he's her closest friend.

TikTok career

She joined TikTok in 2018. The next year, she began to upload content to Instagram. Today, she has more than 5.6 million fans on the former platform, over 690,000 subscribers on the latter and over 30,000 followers on YouTube.

Just like many other prominent TikTokers, Sofia rose to stardom thanks to lip-sync content. The Walls Could Talk song by Halsey plays in the background of one of her most well-known videos. She created another cool video to the sounds of Bad Guy by Billie Eilish.

She frequently performs in movies and television scenes. Fans appreciated it when she dressed as a mermaid. She creates funny and emotional unboxing videos as well as other types of short-form content.

Sofia has a curvy figure. In many of her photos, she poses on a beach in an elegant bikini.

The star might look like a doll but in fact, she's a down-to-earth and hilarious personality.

Is Sofia Gomez in a relationship?

She used to date Xobrooklynne, a fellow female social media celebrity. In 2022, Sofia explained that they broke up because her partner had sexually assaulted her. After that, Sofia seems to be single.
