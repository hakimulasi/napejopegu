FAQs about Kathleen Quinlan

What is Kathleen Quinlan best known for?

Kathleen Quinlan is renowned for her roles in I Never Promised You a Rose Garden and Apollo 13, earning Academy Award and Golden Globe nominations for the latter.

When did she start her acting career?

She began her acting career with an uncredited role in 1972 and made her credited debut in American Graffiti in 1973.

Has she won any awards for her acting?

Yes, she has won several awards including a Blockbuster Entertainment Award and was nominated for an Academy Award and Golden Globes.

What are some notable films Kathleen Quinlan has appeared in?

Notable films include Apollo 13, The Doors, Event Horizon, and Breakdown. She has had a prolific career in both film and television.

Did she receive any education in acting?

Yes, she studied Drama at College of Marin in California before moving to Los Angeles to pursue acting professionally.

What television roles has she played?

She starred in Family Law and had recurring roles in Chicago Fire, Prison Break, and The Event among others.

Is she married?

She was married to Bruce Abbott from 1994 until their divorce in 2022; they have one son together.

What recent projects has Kathleen Quinlan worked on?

Recent projects include Walking with Herb released in 2021 and a role in the horror film The Stairs in 2020.
