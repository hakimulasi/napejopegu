Paul Wall, Slim Thug &amp; Mike Jones Tease 'Still Tippin' Tour

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Paul Wall, Slim Thug and Mike Jones have teased a “Still Tippin” tour.

Named after the trio’s classic 2004 hit, the tour appeared to be confirmed by the Texas trio in a video posted online.

Wall, Thug and Jones were on the field at the NRG Stadium — home of the Houston Texans — when Jones said: “The tour is official, man.”

Wall then shows the back of Jones’ hoodie which is emblazoned with the words “Still Tippin Tour” and the number 44.

Jones later wrote on Instagram: “Still Tippin Tour is on the way.”

The three Houston legends performed at halftime during the Texans’ playoff win against the Cleveland Browns on Saturday night (January 13).

“Still Tippin” was released in November 2004 as the lead single from Mike Jones’ debut album Who Is Mike Jones? It has since been certified platinum while becoming a staple in the canon of classic Hip Hop songs.

The producer wrote on X earlier this month: “just sent @paulwall some heaters,” revealing he sent him 15 beats before repeatedly praising the Southern rap legend.
