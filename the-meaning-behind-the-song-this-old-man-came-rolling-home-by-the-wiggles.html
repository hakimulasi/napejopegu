Frequently Asked Questions about “This Old Man Came Rolling Home”

The origins of “This Old Man Came Rolling Home” can be traced back to traditional English and Scottish folk music. Over the years, the song has evolved and been adapted by different artists, including The Wiggles, who added their own unique twist to it.

“This Old Man Came Rolling Home” is popular among children due to its catchy melody and interactive nature.
